How to Perform Hajj and Umrah

Put on the Ihram before the Miqaat

Say the intention for Ihram and Hajj

Do tawaf 7 times

Offer 2 rakah salah behind maqam e Ibrahim

Drink Abe Zam Zam

Make saee 7 times

Cut your hair shorter

Take off your Ihram and enjoy until the 8th of Dhul Hijjah

On the 8th of Dhul Hijjah after Fajr put on Ihram and make your intention

Go to Mina and pray Zuhr, Asr, Maghreb, Isha, and Fajr shortened and on time

Gather 80 small stones in Mina

Stay the night in Mina

After fajr on the 9th of Dhul Hijjah go to Arafah

In Arafah pray zuhr and asr shortened and combined

In Arafah make dua until Maghreb

After Maghreb go to Muzdelifa

Stay the night in Muzdelifa

After Fajr on the 10th of Dhul Hijjah go to Mina

In Mina throw 7 stones at the big jamaraat

Pray Eid Salah

Offer the sacrifice

Shave your head

Go to Makkah

Make tawaf 7 times

Pray 2 rakah nafl behind Maqam e Ibrahim

Drink Abe Zam Zam

Do saee 7 times

Take off your Ihram

Go to Mina

On the 11th of Dhul Hijjah throw 21 stones at the jamaraat

On the 12th of Dhul Hijjah throw 21 stones at the jamaraat

On the 13th of Dhul Hijjah throw 21 stones at the jamaraat

Before leaving Makkah make tawaf 7 times
